How to:

Use the color picker to select a color. Press "pick" to transfer that color to a section of the palette. Click "random" to get a random color, "clear" to return to black. Cut and paste a six-digit hex value (i.e. #123456) to the picker, press "pick" to transfer to palette.

The Farbtastic Color Picker was used here.
